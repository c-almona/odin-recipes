Indomie Recipe

Homepage

Description

This recipe will walk you through how to make indomie mi goreng noodles like a champ!

Ingredients

  • one packet of indomie mi goreng noodles
  • one egg

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot (enough to boil an egg)
  2. Add egg to pot and boil till desired cook time
  3. pour away some of the water. (you don't need a lot for indomie. unless opting for noodle soup)
  4. Open indomie mi goreng satchet and add the dry noodles to pot
  5. Cook till noodles have separted
  6. Open and add the contents of the seasoning packet and oil
  7. Take out egg and place in bowl of cold water
  8. Once noodles are cooked through, take noodles out and plate
  9. Peel the egg and plate with your now ready dish!
